
The Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) is a terminology used to explain how software is delivered to a customer in a series of steps.
The main aim of SDLC is to Produce a high quality that meets customer requirements(expectations),reaches completion with in time and cost estimates.
The life cycle defines a methodology for improving the quality of software and the overall development process.
